About



Caleb Sanchez is a computational artist and computer programmer. He has studied graphic design and computer programming and will receive his Bachelor's in Computer Science in May from Valparaiso University. He has professional experiences in web development and design creating numerous poster designs, large print art, algorithmic art, as well as designing websites for his university.
